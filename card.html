Tiger

No two tigers have the same stripes,enabling individuals to be identified by their unique pattern of stripes.

  • Scientific name: Panthera tigris
  • Hunting ground: Sunderbans
  • Average lifespan: 20-26 yrs
  • Average speed: 49-65 km/hr

Tiger's are nature's most feared predator.At the top of the food chain they are capable of killing animals twice of their size.Their beautiful orange and black striped coats provide camouflage when hunting prey at night.Tigers are solitary.One simple but effective sentiment which we learn from tigers are:-One small step backward and then a giant leap forward.
